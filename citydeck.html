CityDeck

One of my favorite free places to go in Green Bay is the CityDeck. It's a beautifully maintained area to relax and enjoy the evening by the water. There's even a water sprinkler area where kids can run through the water jets. Honestly, it's just a nice place to relax at the end of long day while enjoying a beautiful sunset.

What to expect

A beautifully designed area, where you can go and relax and enjoy the day. There are a few bars located on the water, so you can enjoy their outdoor patio while you throw back a few cold ones. Hagemeister Park has one of the nicer patio areas on the CityDeck. They even have fires for those chilly fall evenings.

Whether you're just relaxing, drinking, walking, or doing some fishing, the CityDeck is a cool place to check out. It's a nice place to explore the scenery and visit some local shops. One of my favorite features of the CityDeck is that it's free. That's an especially nice feature if you're traveling from out of town since everything can get so expensive.
