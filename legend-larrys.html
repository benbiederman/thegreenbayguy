Legend Larry's

If you're a big wing person like I am, you have to check out Legend Larry's. In my opinion, Legend Larry's is the best wing joint in Green Bay. Their award winning traditional wings are nicely sized and go well with their signature Legend Larry's sauces. Pair that with the fact that Legend Larry's is also a bar, and you have a great combination. You can drink and eat wings all night. If that's not a great time, I don't know what is.

What to expect

When you sit down, you won't be greeted by wait staff, they don't have any. On each table there are some order forms and menus. Once you fill out your order, you'll bring it up to the bar. When you're ordering they'll also ask what you want to drink, if you haven't already ordered a drink. If during your meal you need anything, you'll once again go up to the bar.

Another good thing to call out about Legend Larry's is their menu is pretty limited. Aside from wings, they sell battered shrimp and appetizers. Personally, if you're going to Legend Larry's, go for their award winning wings. Let's be honest, if you go to Legend Larry's and don't get their wings, you're truly missing out in your life.

Listen, I LOVE Legend Larry's, but I still have some criticism for them. My biggest complaint about Legend Larry's is their operation during sporting events. With their current set up, whatever is playing on the jukebox will override the rest of the audio. So, if you're watching a game or other event there, and someone decides they want to hear Cher's entire collection of work, you will listen to that instead of the event that is playing. I learned this the hard way when an NFL draft was interrupted by 80's one hit wonders. It resulted in us having to leave Legend Larry's to watch the draft elsewhere.

Rating: 4.75/5
