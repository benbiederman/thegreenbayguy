Paul B.

For this week's episode of The Green Bay Narrative, Paul joins the show, in person, to share his stories. During our conversation, some of the items we discuss include Lambeau Field Parking, Super Bowl XLV, the NFL Trade Deadline, and so much more. Paul and I had a great time and I was happy to hear his stories. I hope you enjoy it as much as I did.
