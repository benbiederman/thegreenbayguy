Joe O.

On this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I sit down and speak with Joe. Joe is a Minnesota native who loves all Minnesota sports teams, well except one... Joe is a die-hard Packers fan. During my conversation with Joe, we discuss being a Packers fan in Viking territory, the Super Bowl being in his backyard, game day traditions, and much more. I hope you enjoy my conversation with Joe as much as I did.
