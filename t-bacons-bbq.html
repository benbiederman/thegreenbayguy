T. Bacon's BBQ

Green Bay doesn't have too many BBQ restaurants, but the ones we do have seem to do it right. T. Bacon's BBQ is no different. Located inside a gas station casino, T. Bacon's BBQ is serving up BBQ that's smoked daily. Along with their great BBQ, they have their own specialty BBQ sauce that seem to be house-made as well.

What to expect

As I stated above, T. Bacon's BBQ is located inside a gas station casino, with that there are some negatives. The biggest of which is that there were times when I got a whiff of cigarette smoke. As much as I love my meat smoked, I don't enjoy cigarette smoke with it. I wouldn't let that discourage you from trying it though.

Their BBQ is made each and every morning. The sweet smell of smoked meat fills the air each and every day as a signal to remind people where to eat lunch. Very similar to the Bat Signal, haha. Their sauces are made in-house and have their own unique flavors.

Cigarette smoke aside (which is out of T. Bacon's control), T. Bacon's BBQ was a good spot to eat. If you're looking for some BBQ in Green Bay, go check out T. Bacon's BBQ.

*UPDATE* Since writing my initial review, T. Bacon's BBQ has relocated. They are now in their own location, no longer shared with the casino, thus no more cigarette smoke impacting their food. I'm happy they made this move as it will make this experience more enjoyable. This move also puts them close to Lambeau Field for anyone who is looking to for food options near the stadium. I'm looking forward to trying T. Bacon's BBQ again and giving it another review.

Rating: 4.0/5
