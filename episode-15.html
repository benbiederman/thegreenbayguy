Episode 15

Landon K.

For this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I sit down and speak with one of the newest members of the Packers equipment team. Landon is from Illinois but a die-hard Packer backer. During this episode we discuss how he became a huge Packer fan despite being an Illinois native, Packers training camp, Packer game days and so much more. Landon is a super nice guy and it was great to have an opportunity to hear his story. I hope you all enjoy it as much as I did.
