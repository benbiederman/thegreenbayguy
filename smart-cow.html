Smart Cow

If you’re looking for a joint to go to where you can tell yourself the dessert is healthy, Smart Cow may be just for you then. Specializing in self-service frozen yogurt, Smart Cow is located just down the road from Lambeau Field, making it a nice pit stop when you’re in the area. Plus, by going to Smart Cow, you’re helping the community as well. Smart Cow gives a percentage of the proceeds to local schools. You can satisfy your sweet tooth and help the local schools, what more could you ask for?

What to expect

Are you ready to swirl up your own magical concoction consisting of frozen yogurt and other treats? Well, Smart Cow is right up your alley then. It’s a nice family friendly joint to give your kids the freedom to make what they want. They have a nice variety of flavors and extras, and they’re always changing so you don’t get complacent on the options.

Another really cool thing I like, they’re about more than just donating to local schools. At their location, they have books so you and your kids can read while enjoying your dessert if you’d like to. It’s a place you have to check out at least once, if you haven’t been to a self-service frozen yogurt joint before.

Rating: 4.0/5
