Episode 14

Travis F.

During this episode of The Green Bay Narrative I get an opportunity to hear Travis' story. Travis is a huge Packer fan who is from the Carolinas and now resides in Italy. During my conversation with Travis, we discuss a wide-variety of topics ranging from how he became a Packers fan to the difference between a game in the NFL and a game of futbol. This is my longest episode to date and it's for a great reason. Travis had so much to say, I know you'll love this episode like I did.
