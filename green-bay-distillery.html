Green Bay Distillery

Located within walking distance of Lambeau Field is Green Bay Distillery. Green Bay Distillery is known for their Wisconsin style dishes like the cheesecurd burger (pictured above), pretzel with beer cheese, and tailgater pizza. To compliment their Wisconsin style dishes, they offer a variety of beer from breweries in the region.

What to expect

Green Bay Distillery offers a little bit of something for anyone. Their menu offers a nice variety of choices paired well with a nice drink menu. To go along with their great food, they host a lot of different events throughout the year. During the Packers season some of those events include Inside the Huddle or the Lane Taylor/Kenny Clark Show. All these different selling points make Green Bay Distillery a joint that's worthy trying.

Rating: 4.25/5
