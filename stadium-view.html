Stadium View

Located down the road from the most beautiful place in Green Bay, Lambeau Field, is a bar with one of the best views in town. The bar, which holds a very literal name, is one of my favorite bars in town. Part of it has to do with the view and the other part is all the experiences I have had at this bar. You may be asking yourself, “Well, what’s the name of this bar?”. The answer to that question is, of course, Stadium View Bar and Grille.

What to expect

If you're heading to Stadium View during an event at Lambeau Field or even the Resch Center, expect Stadium View to be extremely busy. Stadium View is currently wrapping up on a remodel that added a lot of indoor/outdoor space, which increases the value it can bring to the customers.

During this trip, we elected to sit outside for our meal. They offer a nice outdoor seating area where you can have food or drinks. Outside they had a DJ, DJ Dubz, spinnin' up some hits for those of us enjoying the outdoor scene. DJ Dubz had some people feeling the music he was putting out. On a side note, I thought it was awesome that this kid is out there chasing his passions. I'm terrible at guessing ages, but he's maybe 12 or 13 years old. So for me, it was so cool to see him just hustling to pursue something he loves.

I elected to try their French dip on this visit to Stadium View, you know, because when it's 85 degrees out and you're sitting in the hot sun, a nice hot sandwich with a hot dipping sauce is the perfect item to order. Despite my poor life decision, it was a great sandwich.

If you're looking for a spot to have some drinks and some food and want to stay relatively close to the action of Lambeau Field, Stadium View is a great spot for you. I'd happily go back again.

Rating: 4.25/5
