Episode 13

Peter B.

On this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I sit down and speak with a great Packer fan named Peter. Peter resides in beautiful Denmark (the country, not Denmark, Wisconsin). During my episode with Peter, we find out how you become a Packers fan being from a European country, his trips to Lambeau Field, his game day rituals, and so much more. Peter was also nice enough to give some tips on how to make some great smoked food for your game day. I'm grateful Peter took the time to tell his story and hope you enjoy his story as much as I did.
