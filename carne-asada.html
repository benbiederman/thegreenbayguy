Carne Asada

What's needed:

  • - 1 cup of cilantro chopped
  • - 3 large cloves of garlic, minced
  • - 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • - 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • - Juice of one lime (can add zest as well if you'd like)
  • - 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • - 2 pounds of skirt or flank steak.

Directions:

  • 1. Combine all ingredients except steak in a ziplock bag or large bowl, mix together thoroughly.
  • 2. Add the steak to the marinade; be sure that the steak is coated well in marinade.
  • 3. Allow steak to rest for 1 - 4 hours in the refrigerator.
  • 4. Heat up grill and cook the steak until your desired doneness is achieved.
  • 5. Remove from the grill and allow for steak to rest for 5 minutes before cutting.
  • 6. Add carne asada to a quesadilla, nachos, burrito, or tacos.
  • 7. Serve and enjoy.
