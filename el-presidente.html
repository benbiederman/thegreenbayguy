El Presidente

Located in downtown De Pere is El Presidente, which if you couldn't tell by the name, is a Mexican restaurant. Known for their authentic Mexican food, El Presidente has been serving the masses for over five years. Get the chips and salsa ready, we're heading into another food review.

What to expect

Like I stated above, El Presidente is located in downtown De Pere, which loves their one-way streets. If you're not from the area, I'd recommend throwing the address into the GPS to make sure you can find it without giving yourself too much of a headache. El Presidente offers a nice range of authentic Mexican dishes. From a pricing standpoint, it seems like El Presidente is a little more expensive than some other Mexican restaurants in the area like El Sarape. The steak burrito I get at El Sarape is like $9 or $10, the one from El Presidente I ordered (not pictured) was $13.

One odd thing that happened during our visit, a person I was dining with ordered shrimp fajitas, her go-to item she orders there. Our food was brought out to us, she noticed that hers didn't look right nor taste like it usually did. At the end of the meal it was brought up that something wasn't right with her dish to our server. The server informed her that they were out of shrimp so they substituted scallops in the dish. It was quite bizarre, in my opinion, that they did that without even consulting the customer. As someone who is a picky eater, that terrifies me, haha.

All-in-all, my food was pretty good and it was a quick turnaround time on the order. I would dine at El Presidente again. I did give them a slightly lower rating due to the whole shrimp/scallop thing though... that's just a bizarre thing to do.

Rating: 3.75/5
