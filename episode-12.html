Episode 12

Jackie S.

On this episode of The Green Bay Narrative I sit down with born and bred Wisconsinite, Green Bay Packer die-hard, Jackie. I start the episode by sharing a funny personal story from the first time I met Jackie, then we jump into Jackie's stories. Find out about what it's like growing up as a Packers fan in Wisconsin, meeting Brett Favre, her first trips to Green Bay, and so much more. It was really fun sitting down with Jackie and hearing her story since Jackie and I have spent many hours talking Packers previously. It was great to hear the official story of Jackie. I hope you all enjoy it as much as I did.
