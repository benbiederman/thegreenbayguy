Episode 23

DuJuan Harris

On this week's episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I am joined by former Packers running back, DuJuan Harris. It was such an awesome opportunity to be able to hear his stories. During the episode we talk about what he's been up to lately, his first touchdown at Lambeau Field, Aaron Rodgers' pranks, and much more. I'm so excited for you to hear this episode. It was a ton of fun to record and I hope you enjoy it as much as I did.
