Lambeau Field

One of the must do things for anyone going to the Green Bay has to be going to Lambeau Field, and I'm not just saying for game day. Lambeau Field is open 363 days a year with so much to do there. I highly recommend going on a tour of the stadium, seeing the Hall of Fame, and checking out the Proshop. You could spend hours at Lambeau Field without running out of things to see and do.

What to expect

Lambeau Field is busy year round, sometimes more than others. When it's not football season, they host a lot of events at Lambeau Field. However, don't let that deter you from checking it out; I mean, Lambeau Field is built to hold 80,000 people. It's a perfect place to explore around and take in the history of the stadium.

If you're looking to take a stadium tour and/or Hall of Fame tour, you can guarantee you'll have tickets by buying online in advance.

Also, I'd go out on a limb and say don't go to 1919 Kitchen & Tap for anything more than a drink or some cheesecurds. It's an absolutely stunning atmosphere, but the food in my opinion is overpriced and not great quality.
