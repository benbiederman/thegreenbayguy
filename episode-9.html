Episode 09

Corey P.

The story of how you became a fan of your team is always an interesting one, even more so when that team you're a fan of isn't available to you where you reside. In this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, we speak with Corey who resides in beautiful Australia. During this episode we discuss how Corey became a fan being from Australia, his first trip to Green Bay, game day traditions given the huge timezone different and much more. I'm going to also go on the record and say Corey has the best accent we've heard thus far.
