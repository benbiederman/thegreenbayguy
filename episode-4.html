Episode 04

Dave C.

For this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I get to hear Dave's stories, and boy, does he have some good ones. Dave resides in sunny Arizona, but proudly represents the Packers. This die-hard Packers fan takes at least two trips a year to beautiful Lambeau Field. In this episode, find out how Dave became a fan of the Packers, his experience meeting Bart Starr, the work around to getting to watch every Packers game prior to the existence of Sunday Ticket, and much more. Dave had some hilarious stories, some that may leave you in disbelief.
