Voyageur Park

Get your padlock and be ready to seem like a true romantic to your significant other. Located in beautiful De Pere Wisconsin, Voyageur Park is a nice area to walk around and enjoy a beautiful Wisconsin day. There's a small pier you can walk out to, which has padlocks all over it displaying initials of hopeless romantics. Voyageur Park is one of my go-to spots when my girlfriend and I want to go enjoy the outdoors, not because we bring a lock, but because it's a nice place to walk.

What to expect

One thing to expect is a nice walking trail that leads you to Voyageur Park. You can walk throughout the park, which then will lead you out to a pier. If you time it right, you can see a small canal in use. It's quite interesting to stand on the bridge and watch them go through the process of raising/lowering the water for boats that are out traveling that day. It's a great spot for a picnic as well, if you wanted to go that route. There's a huge yard in the middle of the park where you can relax or partake in the yoga or other activities that go on there periodically.

Another nice part about walking at Voyageur Park is that you can take the walking path down to Zesty's to get yourself a small treat. I mean, you're out there walking around, you deserve a nice dessert! Go enjoy yourself a beautiful Wisconsin day at a nice park.
